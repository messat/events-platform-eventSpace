Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our event management platform ("Event Space") integrated with Google Calendar.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Calendar Data: We only request access to your calendar for adding and managing events you create through our platform.
  • Personal Information: Such as your Google profile name and email address (if provided during authentication).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To add events to your Google Calendar upon your request.
  • To manage and display events you have booked through our platform.

4. Data Sharing and Security

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • All calendar data is stored securely and is only used for providing the service.

5. Google API Disclosure

Our use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Your Choices

  • You can revoke our access to your Google Calendar at any time via your Google Account settings.
